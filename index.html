Q) What Is Juturna?

A) Juturna is the studio debut album by American rock band 'Circa Survive'.

Q) When was Juturna released?

A) Juturna was released on April 19, 2005.


Notes: The end of the album has a hidden track now known as "House of Leaves". The hidden song could be found at 8:56 mark of "Meet Me in Montauk".

The song had no official title until the release of "10th Anniversary Deluxe Edition of Juturna", which features the demo of the hidden track as a it's own song.


Cultural References:

The album was inspired by the movie 'Eternal Sunshine of the Spotless Mind'.


"Oh, Hello", "In Fear and Faith", and "Meet Me in Montauk" all reference lines from the movie
