Esao Andrews


Esao Andrews is a Japanese-American artist and freelance illustrator who is known for his surreal and dark paintings that often feature mysterious and erotic imagery.
He received his B.F.A. in NYC from The School of Visual Arts in 2000 and has established a career both in the fine art and commercial world.

The artist is best known for his album artwork for the band Circa Survive, as well as his comic book covers, skateboards designs, and mural work.
He is influenced by artists such as Gustav Klimt, Egon Schiele, Alfons Mucha, and Joe Sorren.

Andrews currently lives in Los Angeles with his girlfriend, dog, and rabbit.

Source: Conversation with Bing, 7/23/2023
